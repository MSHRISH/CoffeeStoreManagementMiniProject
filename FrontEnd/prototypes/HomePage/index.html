Welcome to Kappi Kadai, where every cup tells a story. Nestled in Madurai, our coffee shop is a haven for coffee lovers seeking a blend of quality and community. Whether you're craving a rich espresso, a velvety latte, or a soothing herbal tea, our passionate baristas craft each drink with care. Pair your beverage with our freshly baked pastries or artisanal sandwiches for a delightful experience. Join us for a moment of warmth and connection at Kappi Kadai.
